About Me

I am a dedicated Lecturer in Economics at the University of Sahiwal, Pakistan, with academic background in economic theory and applied economics. My work focuses on Development economics, Monetary policy, Applied econometrics, & Climate change.

I have contributed to multiple peer-reviewed publications, taught undergraduate and graduate courses, and engaged in academic and professional development activities across Pakistan.
